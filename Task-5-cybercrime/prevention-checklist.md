## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
# General Safety
- Create unique, complex passwords for every individual account you own.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all essential apps, especially for banking, social media, and email.
- Avoid tapping on suspicious links sent via text, email, or messaging apps like WhatsApp and Instagram.
- Verify the sender’s identity before you ever reveal personal details or login credentials.
- Stay current with updates for your phone’s operating system, apps, and web browsers to patch security gaps.
- Refrain from accessing sensitive accounts while connected to public or unsecured Wi-Fi networks.
- Always sign out of your accounts if you happen to use a shared or public computer.
- Stick to official app stores and verified developers when downloading new software to your devices.

# UPI/ FINANCIAL SAFETY
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, CVV, or bank password with anyone.
- Remember: to receive money, you do not need to enter your UPI PIN or scan a QR code.
- Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown people or sellers.
- Always check UPI payment requests twice before approving them.
- Use only official payment apps such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or your bank’s app.


# Reporting
- If you suspect cybercrime or online fraud, report it at cybercrime.gov.in.
- For urgent financial fraud, call 1930 immediately.
